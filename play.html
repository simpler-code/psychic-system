How to Play

Click on any of your pieces to see its available moves highlighted on the board. Green dots indicate safe squares, while capture opportunities are shown with a ring indicator. Click a highlighted square to move your piece there.

About the AI

Our chess engine uses the minimax algorithm with alpha-beta pruning and quiescence search to deliver a challenging opponent rated at approximately 2100 ELO. It evaluates positions using piece-square tables and understands tactical nuances like forks, pins, and discovered attacks.

Special Moves

  • Castling — Move your king two squares toward a rook to castle kingside (O-O) or queenside (O-O-O). Available if neither piece has moved and the path is clear.
  • En Passant — Capture an opponent's pawn that has just moved two squares forward as if it had moved only one.
  • Pawn Promotion — When a pawn reaches the far rank, it automatically promotes to a Queen.

Game Modes

Choose White to move first and control the initiative, or choose Black to play defensively and let the AI make the first move. You can start a new game at any time by clicking the "New Game" button.
