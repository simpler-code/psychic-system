Getting Started

New to chess? Follow these simple steps to begin your journey.

  1. Learn the Rules — Visit our Learn Chess page to understand how each piece moves, special rules like castling and en passant, and how games are won or drawn.
  2. Play Your First Game — Head to Play Chess and choose your color. Click any piece to see its legal moves highlighted on the board.
  3. Solve Puzzles — Train your tactical eye with our Chess Puzzles. Start with mate-in-1 and work your way up.
  4. Study Openings — Browse our Opening Explorer to learn popular first moves and their strategic ideas.
