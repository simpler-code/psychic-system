About Chess Beast

A free, open chess platform built for players of all skill levels.

Our Mission

Chess Beast was created with a simple mission: make high-quality chess training accessible to everyone, completely free. Whether you're a complete beginner learning how the knight moves, or an experienced player looking to sharpen your tactics, our platform provides the tools you need to improve.

What We Offer

  • Play vs AI — Challenge a sophisticated chess engine rated at approximately 2100 ELO. The AI uses minimax search with alpha-beta pruning to deliver a strong, realistic opponent.
  • Interactive Puzzles — Train your tactical vision with mate-in-1 and mate-in-2 puzzles. Immediate feedback helps you learn from every attempt.
  • Comprehensive Learning Resources — From basic rules to advanced strategy, our guides cover everything you need to know about chess.
  • Opening Explorer — Browse a database of popular openings with move sequences and strategic explanations.

Technology

Chess Beast is built entirely with modern web standards — HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript. No downloads, no plugins, no accounts required. The site works on any device with a web browser, from desktop computers to smartphones.

Our chess engine runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. It implements a complete move generator with support for all chess rules including castling, en passant, and promotion. The evaluation function uses piece-square tables tuned for positional play, while the search algorithm employs iterative deepening alpha-beta with quiescence search to find strong moves efficiently.

Why Free?

We believe chess is one of humanity's greatest intellectual treasures and should be accessible to everyone regardless of financial situation. The platform is supported by advertising, allowing us to keep all features free while maintaining and improving the service.
